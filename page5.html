Does Numbuh 3 Have a Secret Admirer?

Has the lovable agent been swept away for good?

Prepare for a love story for the ages! Recently it's been reported that Numbuh 3 has been receiving large amounts of her favorite snack: popcorn, but no one has stepped forward to claim responsibility. With the treat being left right at her doorstep, the force is left confused. Could this gifter be a member of KND? Are they possibly a high ranking member of Sector V? You won't believe the truth!
